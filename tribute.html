Here's a time-line of Steve Jobs life


  • 1971: Birthdate - Born on 28th June 1971 in South Africa.
  • 1991: Blastar - Blastar was a game written by Elon Musk in 1984 (at the age of 12). The source code was published in a magazine and he received $500 for it.
  • 1999: X.com - Musk invested some of his windfall from the Zip2 sale into his next venture: X.com, an online bank, launched with three other co-founders.
  • 2000: Paypal - X.com merged with software company Confinity just one year later, forming PayPal, a secure online payments platform with a foundation in payments conducted for PalmPilots.
  • 2002: Space X - Musk next set his sights on a lofty goal: space exploration, and the future colonization Mars.
  • 2004: Tesla - Musk entered a Series A funding round with an investment of $6.5 million, and eventually took an increasingly active role in the company. He has been CEO since 2008.
  • 2006: Solar City - Founded by his cousins in 2006, SolarCity received Musk’s patronage from the offset; he was their primary financial backer.
  • 2013: Hyperloop -Musk unveiled a proposal for a new form of transportation between the Greater Los Angeles Area and the San Francisco Bay Area.
  • 2016: Nueralink - Another research endeavor, Musk co-founded Neuralink in 2016 with the goal of working on “brain-machine interfaces,” or BMIs, that can be implanted directly into the body.
  • 2016: The Boring company - Founded with the intention of helping dispel city traffic via underground tunnels as a subsidiary of SpaceX, the Boring Company is one of Musk’s side projects. Its aim: build tunnels.
